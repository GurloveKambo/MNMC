New Patients

We Welcome New Patients

We are always accepting new patients! Simply book an appointment with one of our doctors through HealthEngine or HotDoc, by calling (03) 6144 6499, or emailing info@mtnelsonmedicalcentre.com.au.

On the day of your appointment, you can complete the New Patient Registration Form at our clinic. We look forward to welcoming you and providing excellent care.

  • Bring your Medicare card
  • Bring any current medications
  • Arrive 10 minutes early for paperwork
  • Bring any recent pathology or imaging results

Transparent Pricing

✓ Bulk Billing Available

Bulk Billing for All Valid Medicare Cardholders

We are proud to bulk-bill all patients who hold a valid Medicare card. This means standard GP consultations have no out-of-pocket cost for eligible patients.

  • Standard GP consultations — $0 gap fee with valid Medicare
  • Children's consultations — bulk billed
  • Aged care patients — bulk billed
  • Pensioners and concession cardholders — bulk billed

* Some specialised procedures or after-hours services may incur additional costs. Please ask our reception team for details.

My Medicare

Register Your Regular GP

My Medicare is a voluntary patient registration scheme that helps connect you to a regular GP clinic for ongoing, continuous care. Registering is free and takes just minutes.

Registering with Mt Nelson Medical Centre means you have a dedicated home clinic supporting your long-term health. You can register via the Medicare app, MyGov, or ask our reception team.
